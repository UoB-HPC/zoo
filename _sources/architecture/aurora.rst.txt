NEC SX-Aurora TSUBASA
=====================

Our NEC SX-Aurora node is configued with 2 Vector Engines with one single-socket Skylake Vector Host.

Introductory material to this architecture can be found in NEC's `First Steps Guide`_

.. _First Steps Guide: https://sx-aurora.github.io/posts/VE-first-steps/

Compiler
--------
The NEC compilers are available in the ``nec/2.3.0`` module.
This module will only work on the node; the software is not available on the login node.
